One designer's path to becoming a creative director

"At age 9 I decided I would make design apart of my career no matter what."

I am a content designer and a digital storyteller. I believe in not giving a presentation, but giving a performance. My name is Melanie Tam and I am a seasoned designer who strives to learn multiple disciplines of design. The reason why I am so passionate about design is that it creates dialogue and experiences between people. My goal is to create universal designs shared by memories and experiences for everyone.

A designer's path is very comparable to a marathon runner. Design is a skill and just like a marathon runner, runners can take up to 3-6 months to train for a 26.2 mile race. You will not get there in one day and if you do, trust me you will probably not run a race again for a long time. As a designer we are creators or the unknown. We design to problem solve and some may even call us inventors. Growing up, design was never seen as a "real job" in my household. I never met up to my familie's expectation of becoming a doctor, pharmacist or nurse, but I told myself at age 9 that I would do whatever it takes to make design apart of my career. My design journey has been so incredible with constance increase of growth. I hope to mentor and guide other young designers who have been in my path into helping them reach their design goals. Anything is possible. You just need to believe in yourself and take the first step towards your goals.
